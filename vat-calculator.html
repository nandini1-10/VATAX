VAT Tips

  • VAT is charged on the supply of goods and services in Bangladesh
  • Businesses with annual turnover above ৳80 lakh must register for VAT
  • VAT returns must be submitted monthly by the 15th of the following month
  • Keep proper records of all VAT transactions for compliance
