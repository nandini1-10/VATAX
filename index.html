What are the current VAT rates in Bangladesh?

The standard VAT rate in Bangladesh is 15%. However, there are reduced rates of 7.5%, 5%, and 0% for specific goods and services as per NBR regulations.

What are the tax-free income limits for FY 2024-25?

For FY 2024-25, the tax-free income limit is ৳350,000 for general taxpayers. For women, senior citizens (65+), and disabled persons, the limit is ৳400,000.

How is VAT calculated on inclusive vs exclusive basis?

VAT can be calculated on exclusive basis (VAT added to base price) or inclusive basis (VAT included in price). Our calculator supports both methods with clear options.

What investment deductions are allowed?

You can claim deductions for investments in approved savings certificates, life insurance premiums, contributions to recognized provident funds, and investments in government bonds.
